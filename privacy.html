Drone Salvo — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Drone Salvo is a standalone offline strategy game developed by Donovan Farrell. Your privacy is important. This policy explains what data the app does and does not collect.

Data We Collect

Drone Salvo does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no internet connection, no user accounts, no analytics, and no third-party SDKs of any kind.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following data locally on your device using Apple's standard UserDefaults mechanism:

  • Your total number of wins
  • Your total number of losses
  • Your best game score (fewest strikes to win)

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer or any third party.

Third-Party Services

Drone Salvo does not use any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or crash reporting tools.

Children's Privacy

Drone Salvo does not collect any data from users of any age, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in a future version of the app, the updated policy will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.
